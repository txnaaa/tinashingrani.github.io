Welcome!

Hi, I'm Tina a creative ICT & Media student from Curaçao, studying in Eindhoven. This portfolio shows my work from semester 3, including school projects and professional activities.

How to Use This Portfolio

1. Choose a Project

From the home page, click on any project card:

  • Catchee — A digital speed meet game for young adults
  • Portfolio — This portfolio and personal development
  • Bikini Bottom — An interactive map passion project

2. Explore Learning Outcomes

Each project has 5 learning outcomes (LO 1-5). Click the buttons at the top to switch between them:

  • LO 1: User interaction
  • LO 2: User interaction
  • LO 3: Software design & realization
  • LO 4: Professional skills
  • LO 5: Personal leadership

3. View the Work

Each learning outcome shows:

  • What I did
  • How I did it (process)
  • What I learned (reflection)
  • Links to images, videos, and documents

4. Navigate Back

Use the ← Back button or the menu to return to the home page or switch projects.

About This Portfolio

This portfolio combines my love for design with my ICT skills. The visual style is inspired by Rituals' "Ritual of Amsterdam" collection, mixing Dutch and Japanese aesthetics with Delft blue colors and clean design.

All links are underlined. Click them to see images, videos, or documents that show my work in detail.
